Services

Legal


• Corporate Law : Our team handles corporate and related matters, including incorporation and management of companies both in Cyprus and internationally. It offers the full range of legal and advisory services in the field including corporate acquisitions, registrations, corporate finance and secured financing agreements, corporate reorganizations, mergers and restructuring, corporate investigations and due diligence reports, international corporate transactions, stock exchange listings and redomiciliations.
•Funds actively involved in providing comprehensive legal solutions for the creation of EU-regulated, Cyprus-domiciled investment funds i.e. ICIS (International Collective Investment Schemes) and UCITS (Undertaking for Collective Investment Schemes in Transferable Securities), as well as EU-regulated Cyprus-domiciled financial services companies i.e. CIF (Cyprus Investment Firm) regulated by Cypriot Securities and Exchange Commission.
• Commercial contracts drafting and review
• Immigration Law : assisting our clients throughout the process of applying and issuance of work visas, residence permits and citizenship by investment.

Trusts & Trustee Services

Since 2010, our dedicated team of experts offers unique and tailor-made solutions ensuring that the proper attention is applied to each client according to their individual needs and expectations. We apply strict confidentiality in regards to the formation and administration of trusts, and offer Trustee and/or Protector services as required and requested.

Our services include but are not limited to:


• The Formation of International Trusts under Cypriot Law
•The Provision of Trustee and Protector services
•The administration of Trusts
•Legal consultation on any Trust related matter
•The re-domicilation of Trusts from and to Cyprus

International Tax Planning

Our team of experts and tax law specialists offers tailor-made solutions for all issues that our clients’ business is facing. Our services include:
- Reviewing and advising on existing or new structures
- Advising on cross-border tax structuring for international investments and transactions
- Advising on Double Tax Treaties and EU Law
- Consulting on tax related matters of cross border mergers and acquisitions
- Consultation on existing or new structures
- Identifying efficient jurisdictions for structuring international investments and transactions
- Transfer Pricing advisory
- Substance support on issues with Cyprus Tax Office
- Tax Reports & Rulings
- Tax Opinions
- Tax Clearance
- Personal taxation
- Capital Gains Tax

Accounting
